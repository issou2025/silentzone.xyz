Detailed Review

Design and Build Quality

The WH-1000XM5 features a completely redesigned aesthetic compared to its predecessor. Sony has opted for a cleaner, more minimalist look with a seamless headband and redesigned ear cups. The build quality is exceptional, with premium materials throughout including soft synthetic leather and high-quality plastics.

Noise Cancellation Performance

This is where the WH-1000XM5 truly shines. Sony's V1 processor works in conjunction with the QN1 chip to deliver the most effective noise cancellation we've ever tested. Whether you're on a plane, in a busy office, or walking down a noisy street, these headphones create an impressive cone of silence around your ears.

Sound Quality

The audio performance is outstanding across all genres. The 30mm drivers deliver rich, detailed sound with excellent separation. Bass is punchy without being overwhelming, mids are clear and present, and highs are crisp without being harsh. The default tuning is well-balanced, but you can customize the sound through Sony's Headphones Connect app.

Battery Life and Charging

With 30 hours of battery life with ANC on, the WH-1000XM5 offers excellent endurance. The quick charge feature provides 3 hours of playback from just 3 minutes of charging, making these headphones perfect for travel.

Comfort and Fit

Despite the premium build, these headphones are surprisingly lightweight and comfortable for extended wear. The ear cups provide good seal without excessive clamping force, and the headband distributes weight evenly.
